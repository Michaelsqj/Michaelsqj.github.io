velocity-selective labeling

  1. velocity above $v_c$ is labeled
  2. then velocity above $v_c$ is crushed
  3. target the spin from above $v_c$ to below $v_c$

Disadvantage:

SNR penalty, diffusion & eddy distortion, only those move along gradient direction can be labeled

not practical
